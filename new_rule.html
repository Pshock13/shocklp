Why would I need to do this?

When submitting FPY reports to phl7-fpyreports@amazon.com, you tend to end up with a cluttered inbox that's hard to tell reports from your own personal e-mails. This short process is going to keep the two seperated.

Decluttering your inbox
Note: Proceed at your own risk
These instructions are meant to help you, and if followed correctly, should improve on your quality of life.
However, if something does go wrong reach out to shocklp and I'll help as best I can.

Open Outlook

The first step is pretty simple. It's as easy as pressing the window key on your keyboard or hitting the start button on your task bar and then typing in the searchbar for "outlook".

Make a New Rule

To make a new rule, you'll first need to locate in your inbox, a previous FPY-Report from the email address phl7-fpyreports@amazon.com.

Once you've located one, right-click on the email and hover over "Rules" from the menu that pops up.

Hovering over "Rules" will slide open another menu (Figure 1) where you will click on "Always Move Messages To:phl7-fpyreports@amazon.com".

From here you'll need to...

Create a New Folder

In the new dialog box the pops up after selecting "Always Move Messages To:phl7-fpyreports@amazon.com", select the button on the right labeled "New...". (Figure 2.1)

This brings up the "Create New Folder" dialog box.
In the "Name:" input box, put any name that you would like your folder to be. (Figure 2.2) I simply named mine FPY-Reports as that is going to be the only thing going in this folder, but you can also use this technique to seperate other things as well.
I also selected for that new folder to go inside my Inbox folder, you can place it wherever you'd like.

Once you have all that done, press [OK]

Run the New Rule

Now you want to get that new rule to work for you.

You've created a new folder for the rule to run in, go ahead and select that folder and press [OK] again and you're set! (Figure 3)

It might take a minute or to to move all the previous e-mails you've received, but from now on all of your FPY-Reports will be sent to that folder you created.
