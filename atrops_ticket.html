First copy the ATROPS shipment ID
Head over to RIVER

Go over to river.amazon.com and select "No Ship Method U_002" to start the ticketing process.

Paste that ShipID into the input
Press "Submit Ticket"

Here you will see the reason for the kick out (if any) and the solution

Press "Submit Ticket" again

This will show you a preview of what the ticket will look like once submitted

Copy the link to the ticket

Right-click "view ticket" and select "Copy Link Location" (Or press a)

Alternatively, click "view ticket" and then copy the entire address bar when that opens up.

Paste the link in the notes on RODEO

Click on the little pen icon next to one of the items in the shipment to open a note. Then select "OK" fo Flag Type. Finally, paste the link to the ticket in the "note:" box and click the yellow "Save" button.
